Forest:-

Forests are vast areas of land dominated by trees and other woody vegetation.
They play a crucial role in:

1. Producing oxygen: Forests are responsible for producing a significant portion of the world's oxygen.
2. Supporting biodiversity: Forests are home to a vast array of plant and animal species.
3. Regulating climate: Forests help regulate the climate by absorbing carbon dioxide and producing oxygen.
4. Providing resources: Forests provide wood, fuel, and other essential resources.
